Our Fish Cutlet Recipe !

Ingredients

Boiled potatoes – 300g
Tin fish – 250g
Eggs – 02
Bread crumbs – 100g
Green chilies – 02
Onions – 100g
Curry leaves
Salt to taste
Pepper powder – ½ tsp
Lime to taste
Oil for deep frying

Instructions

Peel off the boiled potatoes and mash them until it becomes a paste. Do same to tin fish. Chop green chilies, onions and curry leaves. Heat oil in a pan and fry chopped chilies, onions and curry leaves a bit. Add salt and pepper as per your taste. Add tin fish and mix well to combine. Add some lime to taste and mix. Finally add mashed potatoes and mix thoroughly. Let this cutlet mixture cool down around 05 mins. Make small cutlet balls from this mixture. From these ingredients we can make around 20-25 medium sized balls. First dip these balls in beaten egg and then coat with bread crumbs. Finally, heat oil and fry cutlet until they become golden brown.
