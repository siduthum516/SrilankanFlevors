Our Pani Walalu Recipe !

Ingredients

Wheat Flour 50g
Rice Flour 250g
Undu Flour 250g
Coconut Oil 1/2 (For Frying)
Coconut Milk 1/2 Tea Cup
Water 1/2 Tea Cup
Sugar 150g

Instructions

Dunk Undu Flour about 6 hrs and crush. Then mix Rice flour, Wheat flour to The Undu Flour. After mixing leave it about another 6 hrs. After 6 Hrs add coconut milk and make a pour proportion. (like the mixture of String hoppers) Add sugar to the water and boil, Then keep it in warm. Take a handkerchief and make a little hole. Add oil to a frying pan and heat well. Get the handkerchief which we made a hole and sink it in the mixture and pour roundly to the heated oil. When it getting the golden color put it in the warm honey we made with sugar and water. Take it in one minute.
