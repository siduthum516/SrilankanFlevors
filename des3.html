Our Pani Aluva Recipe !

Ingredients

500 Grams Rice Flour
375 Millilitre Coconut Treacle
3 Tablespoon Sugar
2 Cardamom Pods (Crushed without shell)
100 Grams Cashew Nuts

Instructions

First, put the rice flour in to a pan on a medium heat. Keep stirring for about 6-7 minutes, until the flour becomes light brown in colour . Turn off the heat and keep the flour in a separate bowl. Prepare the tray by spreading some rice flour on the surface. And keep some more (1-2 tablespoons) of rice flour aside. Now add the treacle and sugar in to a saucepan on a medium heat and start stirring for about 3 minutes. add the cardamom and mix well for another minute. Now add the cashew nuts and stir for another minute. Turn off the heat and start adding the rice flour bit by bit while mixing. Combine the flour well in to the treacle mixture. Now spread it on to the floured tray. Level evenly and add the separated rice flour on the Aluva surface. Now cut straightaway in to pieces using a knife.
