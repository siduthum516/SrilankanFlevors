Our Milk Toffee Recipe !

Ingredients

Milkmaid - 01 tin
Water – ½ tin
Sugar – 500g
Butter or margarine – 01 tbsp
Vanilla – 01 tsp
Cashew nuts (chopped) – 50g

Instructions

Put milkmaid into a pan. Mix remaining milk with water and put into the pan. Add Sugar. Mix Milkmaid, sugar and water well. Cook on a low fire, stirring continuously until mixture leaves the pan. (As milk and sugar tend to get clumpy & sticky when cooking, make sure to stir often.) Then, add vanilla and nuts. Spread this mixture in a greased pan and cut it into desired shapes before it gets harden. Keep it aside until milk toffee becomes harden and cool. Store in an air tight container .
